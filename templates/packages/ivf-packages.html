What is IVF package?

The IVF package is used by fertility clinics to create a special or limited offer for clients who are looking for the most interesting IVF offer on the market. For an IVF package, the customer pays one payment, which usually includes the main steps of the IVF process, as well as, for example, special procedures, accommodation, transport or medication.
