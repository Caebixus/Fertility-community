What is Guest Blogging and how to use it?



Guest blogging guidelines:

  • Posts should be between 800-1200 words in length and in English.
  • A paid PRO plan can have only one article and one author. The PREMIUM paid plan can have up to five articles and up to five authors.
  • The most popular articles are those with a friendly, conversational tone.
  • Send us something value-packed. Your piece should be actionable and insightful. While the post doesn’t have to be ‘ground breaking’, it should not be ‘common knowledge’ or obvious. Encourage readers to reexamine what they know and allow them to consider things in a new light. You’re the experts, give us your unique spin!
  • Double check it. We only accept guest posts that use correct spelling, grammar, and punctuation. Entries that require excessive editing cannot be accepted.
  • The post you are submitting must be your own, 100% original content, exclusive to us. It should not be posted elsewhere (including on your own blog/website) before or after it appears on our site. This is creating duplicate content on internet and we both don't want that.
  • Your article must be presented in a non-advertorial, non-promotional fashion. You are welcome to make mention of (and link to) your clinic or other landing page, once within the body.
  • We only accept topics related to infertility treatment, unique IVF technologies or services regarding your clinic. You can always contact us ahead.
  • We reserve the right not to accept the article.
  • If you cancel your subscription and have active blogposts - these blogposts will no longer be published on the location pages or in the detail of your clinic. However, the blogpost web address will still be accessible to you or anyone else.
  • If you want to change your active and already published article - contact us.
