Why You Should Choose IVF in the Czech Republic


The Czech Republic is a notable travel destination for many couples and singles looking for a reputable location for their IVF treatments abroad. The top reasons to choose the Czech Republic include:

  1. Low costs compared to other IVF clinics in Europe

    One of the main reasons many people opt for IVF in the Czech Republic is the low costs associated with the treatment at clinics. Therefore, the Czech Republic is an excellent option if you are looking for an affordable location to undergo IVF, yet you don’t want to compromise on the quality of your treatment or the safety of the procedure.

  2. Anonymous egg donations and short waiting time for egg donors

    By law, all eggs are donated anonymously by donors, and consequently, there is no opportunity for the child to contact the biological mothers. Generally, this is a safer approach as it avoids the children being disappointed when they reach out to a biological parent who would otherwise prefer to remain anonymous.

    This also means that egg donations in the Czech Republic are significantly higher than in countries, such as the UK, which allow children conceived by egg donation to contact biological parents at the age of 18. Therefore, you can expect shorter waiting times when considering IVF with egg donation in the Czech Republic.

  3. Affordable travel costs

    Many people discount the Czech Republic and other more Eastern European countries when travelling from France, the UK or anywhere West of Europe due to the expected costs of travelling to the clinics for treatment and any aftercare needed. However, you may be surprised to hear that travelling to the Czech Republic is more convenient and cheaper than you may presume.

    Flying into Prague, the nation’s capital, is generally not much longer than flying to Germany. The associated costs are lower due to the high level of tourism the country experiences. It is also not expensive to travel by bus to train within the borders once you arrive in Prague.

  4. Less stressful environments which can improve success rates

    Similarly to IVF in Greece, the Czech Republic offers a low-stress environment with clinics making the process as smooth as possible. The surrounding country and laidback cities provide an oasis of calm surrounding your trip. You may even wish to extend your stay to enjoy the attraction of the historical monuments, cultural interest and beautiful mountains and countryside areas that the other tourists appreciate. Of course, the whole experience of your IVF treatment can make a difference in the success rate of your fertility treatment, and your mentality during your visit is no exception.

Unfortunately, the success rates for IVF in the Czech Republic aren’t readily available on a database to the public. Therefore, success rate statistics may not be a factor you can reliably use to decide what country to undergo your IVF treatment.


However, a few statistics have been published on the European Social of Human Reproduction and Embryology (ESHRE), indicating that, generally, success rates are good. Usually, the success rates in clinics in the Czech Republic are even better than those of IVF clinics in countries that are more renowned for their healthcare systems, such as the UK.

IVF Limitations in the Czech Republic


The law in the Czech Republic states that an ‘infertile couple’ constitutes a man and a woman who live together. Therefore, there are limitations for homosexual couples looking to conceive through IVF in the Czech Republic.


Moreover, there are additional limitations for mature women looking to conceive. The Czech Republic states that women receiving IVF with egg donation must be under 48 years and 364 days old.


As mentioned, all donors are anonymous, which may not suit parents who are keen to know a general profile of their sperm or egg donors.
