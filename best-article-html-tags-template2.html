Who They Are

Founded in 1966 by Doc. MUDr. Tonko Mardešić, CSc and a member of MEDICON group, Pronatal is a fertility clinic that provides artificial insemination and genetic examination services to its patients. Pronatal was also the 2nd clinic in Czechoslovakia to have childbirth after IVF.

Pronatal has clinics in six different locations for patients to pick from, including Prague 4, Prague 6, Kolín, České Budějovice, Teplice and Karlovy Vary. These clinics consist of 260 employees who attend to 4000 couples per year. With a team of world-class experts in reproductive medicine and genetics, embryology, gynecology, and obstetrics, urology, and andrology, Pronatal boasts a high success rate.

Since 1986, Doc. MUDr. Tonko Mardešić, CSc, has specialized in the field of sterility diagnosis and treatment. He is also a member of the European Society for Human Reproduction and Embryology (ESHRE), the Executive Committee of ESHRE, the Committee of the Czech Gynecological and Obstetrical Society (ČGPS), and the Section of Assisted Reproduction ČGPS and under his management, Pronatal clinic has achieved great milestones.

 

What They Offer

Pronatal provides assisted reproduction services including IUI, IVF, and IVF with donor cells. They also provide a genetic examination of the couple and embryos. For those who are not ready to become parents – no matter your reason – Pronatal does not leave you out. They offer social freezing until you are ready.

As a non-resident, you would be glad to know that they offer planned accommodation either within the clinic or a nearby hotel. This takes away the need to search for a suitable hotel in an unfamiliar place.

According to reviews from previous patients, Pronatal makes you feel welcome and pays utmost attention to every one of their patients. The Pronatal staff speaks several languages including English, German, Italian, Serbian, Bosnian, and Czech.

According to the laws of the Czech Republic, Pronatal provides services to only heterosexual couples and women below 48 years of age. Average Cost of Treatment

The cost of a complete IVF cycle in Pronatal is 1 700 € and the complete IVF cycle with added laboratory methods goes for 2 400 €, each package comes with specific services included. Some locations have a slight difference in the packages and prices.

In Pronatal Sanatorium and Kolin, the complete IVF cycle with added laboratory methods costs 2 900 € and comes with some inclusions in the package. In Pronatal Spa, Nord, and Repro, the complete IVF cycle with added laboratory methods, medication, and transport costs 3 550 €.

The IVF cycle with donor eggs comes in two packages. ‘Package I’ goes for 4 700 € and Package II goes for 5 150 €. ‘Package II’ comes with Embryo Glue, Time-lapse, and Assisted hatching services. And finally, IVF with donor embryos costs 1 500 € for one embryo and 2 500 € for two embryos.
