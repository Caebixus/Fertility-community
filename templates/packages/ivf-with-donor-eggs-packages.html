What is Egg donation package?

The fertility Egg donation package is used by fertility clinics to create a special or limited offer for clients who are looking for the most interesting IVF with egg donor treatment offer on the market. For an egg donation treatment package, the customer pays one payment, which usually includes the main steps of the fertility treatment process, as well as, for example, special procedures, accommodation, transport or medication.
