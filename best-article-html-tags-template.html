IVF CUBE Clinic is a fertility clinic in Prague that puts the patient at the center of treatment. They emphasize the patient's well-being and tailor treatment to a patient's particular needs. Read on to learn more about this unique clinic.

 

Who They Are

IVF CUBE was founded in 2011. They have a large, dedicated staff led by Hana Visnova, MD, Ph.D., and Renata Huttelova, Ph.D., MBA.

Dr. Visnova has worked in reproductive medicine and obstetrics and gynecology for over 25 years. She is compassionate towards her patients and says that she always tries to treat them as she would her best friends. She's fluent in both English and German.

Dr. Huttelova has also had over 25 years of experience working with IVF. She's a senior ESHRE embryologist and is fluent in English.

 

What They Offer

IVF CUBE offers infertility treatment and also provides infertility testing to help patients better understand where their issues lie. During a patient's first visit, IVF CUBE performs an examination on both the male and female to determine the possible causes of infertility. They determine the best treatment based on the causes.

The main treatments they offer include IVF-ICSI with the patient's own eggs or using donor eggs, sperm, or embryos. They only offer IVF-ICSI, not standard IVF. ICSI injects a single sperm directly into the egg, increasing the chances of fertilization.

In accordance with Czech law, they don't treat same-sex couples, single females, or women over the age of 49. They also require that all donors be anonymous.

 

Success Rates and Other Statistics

IVF CUBE has a cumulative success rate of 85%.

Their success rate by age and use of own eggs vs. use of donor eggs are as follows:

 

IVF With Own Eggs

  • <36: 50%
  • 37-40: 42.5%
  • 40+: 13.6%

 

IVF With Donor Eggs

  • <36: 57.6%
  • 36-40: 54.5%
  • 41+: 50%

 

Around half of IVF CUBE's patients come from abroad. The majority of their foreign patients are from either German or English-speaking countries. Patients come from areas such as the US, the UK, Italy, Ireland, Canada, and Scandinavia. The other half of their patients are from the Czech Republic. IVF CUBE has a contract with public health insurers in the Czech Republic for IVF treatment.

 

Average Cost of Treatment

The average cost for a course of IVF-ICSI (their most standard offering) is €2900 ($3350). Using sperm donors will cost an additional €500 ($580). The total cost for IVF with donor eggs is €5900 ($6800) and the cost to use donor embryos is €1400 ($1620).

Natural IVF is €1200 ($1388) and Mini IVF is €1900($2200). Egg freezing is €2000 ($2300) and sperm freezing is €350 ($400). Embryo freezing is included in the cost of IVF-ICSI.
