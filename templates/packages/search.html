What is fertility treatment package?

The fertility package is used by fertility clinics to create a special or limited offer for clients who are looking for the most interesting fertiltiy treatment offer on the market. For an fertility treatment package, the customer pays one payment, which usually includes the main steps of the fertility treatment process, as well as, for example, special procedures, accommodation, transport or medication.
